/*** self hosting - quick how to ***/
1. Copy this folder "wikiPrevBox" to the root of your website
2. Insert <script src="/wikiPrevBox/wikiPreviewBox.min.js" defer></script> before the </body> tag or inside the <head> tag. 
 
=> read the readme.md for available settings
=> note the comments in the source files
